Welcome!

For the past 15 years, I have had the pleasure to coach leaders, managers and teams from various industries. I've witnessed the impactful benefits of coaching and guided people to reach their full potential. My long experience in Human Resources, coupled with my empathy and positive energy, have made coaching a truly enjoyable experience for both my clients and myself.

I am convinced we never stop learning. I am therefore continuously participating in trainings and have expanded my toolset with Neuroscience certifications. In my free time, I serve as President of the Nyon Chapter of the Soroptimist International, an NGO focused on empowering women worldwide, go on trail runs, and practice yoga.

I look forward to getting to know you.

- Laure

MY CORE VALUES

Loyalty

Loyalty means standing by one's words and fulfilling one's promises. It is essential to building mutual trust in our interactions.

Respect

Respect is the consideration one has for the wellbeing of other people. To respect someone is to give him or her the space to thrive.

Responsibility

To be responsible is to be able to make decisions on one's own. It means standing behind one's commitments. It means taking ownership for one's actions.
