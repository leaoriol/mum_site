Welcome!

For the past 15 years, I have had the pleasure of coaching leaders and teams from various industries. Guiding people to reach their full potential is the benefit of coaching I enjoy the most. My experience in HR, coupled with my empathy and positive energy, have made coaching a truly enjoyable experience for both my clients and myself.

I am convinced we never stop learning. I continuously participate in trainings and have recently earned Neuroscience certifications. In my free time, I serve as President of the Nyon Chapter of the Soroptimist International, a NGO focused on empowering women worldwide, and practice trail running and yoga.

I look very much forward to getting to know you.

- Laure

MY CORE VALUES

Loyalty

Loyalty means being true to your words and meeting your commitments. Loyalty is essential to building mutual trust in our interactions.

Respect

Respect is the consideration one has for the wellbeing of other people. To respect someone is to give importance to the space he or she needs to thrive.

Responsibility

To be responsible is to be able to make decisions on one's own, and subsequently standing behind them. It means taking ownership for one's actions.
