INDIVIDUAL COACHING

You are asking yourself...

  • Do I need to improve my leadership skills?
  • Do I handle change and unpredictable situations well?
  • How can I improve my performance?

Individual Coaching is for you

My role is to help reveal your talents and guide you to develop them. Together, we will clarify your objectives and the obstacles you want to overcome. We will develop solutions and focus on deepening your skills until you are comfortable navigating alone.

What does Individual Coaching look like?

The coaching sessions are tailored to your schedule, with a strategy plan developed at the start and systematically evaluated to ensure your objectives are met.

TEAM COACHING

You are asking yourself...

  • Is our team sufficiently motivated?
  • What vision does our team share?
  • Is our team able to adapt to management changes?
  • How can our team improve its collective practices?

Team Coaching is for you

My role is to help you develop new, stronger collective practices, through concrete activities. Your new team practices will enable you and your team to go from shared objectives to concrete actions. Specific workshops will tackle collective intelligence, team spirit, communication and individual engagement amongst a team.

What does Team Coaching look like?

Team coaching is tailored to your objectives.

It is highly recommended to space out the half-day workshops over time, in order to build on acquired skills.

Three types of workshops are offered and can be adapted to your needs.

COLLECTIVE INTELLIGENCE WORKSHOP

Understand team behaviors and develop its collective intelligence

EFFICIENCY AND VISIBILITY WORKSHOP

Situational activities and concrete exercises focused on communication

EMPOWERMENT WORKSHOP

Guide your team to empower each coworker to be confident, responsible and take appropriate risks

HUMAN RESOURCES CONSULTING

You are asking yourself...

  • How do I retain talents at my company?
  • How can I evaluate my own performance objectively?
  • Can I get a 3rd party opinion to hire the best candidate?

Human Resources Consulting is for you.

My role is to help evaluate the situation and recommand appropriate solutions. Together, we will develop suitable strategies and tools for an optimal solution.

What does HR consulting look like?

HR Consulting is tailored to a particular situation, for businesses or individuals. A strategy plan is developed at the start and systematically evaluated to ensure your objectives are met.

CONSULTING

Get an outside Human Resource expert opinion for a more efficient decision

HIRING ASSESSMENT

External evaluation and situational activities of the best candidates for key positions

PERFORMANCE EVALUATION

Evaluate your performance and skillset for an optimal career change

OUTPLACEMENT

Performance evaluation, followed by guidance and coaching throughout the job searching process
